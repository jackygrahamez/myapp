ISE Homepage Banner

Medium: Flash, Photoshop Size: 482x298 pix These are 2 Flash animations I created for the International Securities Exchange. The 2nd animation was live on ISE's website for 1.5 - 2 years. I was given a flat image and told to animate it with searchlights. Had to create multiple laters in Photoshop and draw in parts of the buildings which were missing to get the 4 searchlights to work as the searchlights needed to be in front of and behind buildings as well as in behind the man/flag.

ISE 3d poster carousel

Medium: Flash, Photoshop, Sound Forge Size: 686x290 pix This is a Flash animation I created for the Internatoinal Securitiess Exchange showcasing their posters. I was given 23 images of various posters and told by ISE that they wanted to create a visually appealing Flash animation that was interactive and displayed their posters. They wanted each poster to have its own description users could click on and created two sound effects for the click on and off. Using a template that I found and a lot of programming, I creataed this Flash 3d carousel using ActionScript, HTML, XML and JavaScript. It gets the user mouse position and rotates content in the opposite direction to the mouse location stopping and populatinig a popup on clicked posters. I recorded this Flash animation using a screen recording program along with mouse interaction.

Clear Sailing Thesis video paths 1 & 2

Medium: Flash, Photoshop, Illustrator, Sound Forge, Cue Base, Final Cut Pro Size: Vector This was my SVA Thesis animation for graduation. It's a choose your own adventure interactive cartoon where the user is given paths each time the roach is attacked. The story has 1 beginning, 2 endings and 16 possible pathways. I came up with a story that used a roach which I knew people would dislike. He travels throughout New York City in search of a way to be reunited with his family at Yankee Stadium. Along the way, he is attacked and the story presents itself with 2 possible directions. I spent 6 months creating this animation doing all storyboarding, drawing, illustration, sound recording and editing, hired a composer, used friends/family for voice talent and programmed it to be interactive in ActionScript. The entire animation consists of 50 Flash swf files stitched together to make 1 long animation about 9 minutes and 45 seconds. In 2001, this video was shown in the Dusty Film Festival at the Ziegfeld Theater in New York City. I recorded this Flash animation using a screen recording program along with mouse interaction.
